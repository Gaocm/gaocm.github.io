About me

Hello ! My name is Congming Gao, and I'm currently a Ph.D student in Computer Science at the Chongqing University , China. 

My research interests gravitate toward Computer Architecture, Emerging Non-Volatile Memory, Flash Memory, and Embedded and Real-Time Systems. I'm also curious about all mathematics and sciences in general. 

In 2014, I obtained a Bachelor Degree in Computer Science at CQU with great honours. 
In 2014.09, I stayed at the same school to complete a Ph.D under the supervision of Dr. Shi , Dr. Liu  and Dr. Edwin Sha  to complete my education. 
Now, I'm a Visiting Scholar with the Department of Electrical andComputer Engineering, University of Pittsburgh under the supervision of Dr. Yang and Dr. Zhang .
