Biography

    Congming GAO is currently an Associate Professor with the School of Information Science, Xiamen University, Xiamen, China. He was a Postdoctoral Researcher with the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University. He received the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science with the College of Computer Science, Chongqing University, Chongqing, China. He was a Research Assistant with the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU), Kowloon, Hong Kong. He was a Visiting Researcher with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Pittsburgh (PITT), Pittsburgh, PA, USA.

Research

    My research topics include Flash Memory, Embedded System and Emerging Non-volatile Memory.
